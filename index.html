Planning

  • Use Pacing Guide to identify Standard to be taught
  • Locate Standard Overview (if a priority standard)
  • Identify Learning Targets and Success Criteria
  • Use CFA, Standard Overview, Unit Plan, and relevant data to plan Instruction
  • Consider strategies to scaffold, access prior knowledge, and engage students

Instruction

  • Deliver high quality, engaging instruction
  • Include daily Checks for Understanding (ex: exit ticket)

Assessment

  • Administer Common Formative Assessment (CFA)
  • Identify students who need reteaching (those who did not master concept per CFA)
  • Identify students needing enrichment

Reteach/Enrich

  • For students needing reteaching, repeat steps 2 and 3
  • For students with mastery, extend learning through enrichment opportunities
